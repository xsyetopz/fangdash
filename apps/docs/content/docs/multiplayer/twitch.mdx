FangDash is built for **Twitch streamers** and their communities. Here's how to use it on stream.

## Signing In

FangDash uses **Twitch OAuth** for authentication. When you sign in, we access:

- Your Twitch username
- Your Twitch avatar

We do **not** access your followers, subscriptions, or any other Twitch data beyond what is needed for authentication.

## Racing Your Chat

As a streamer, create a race room and share the room code with your chat. Viewers join by:

<Steps>
<Step>Going to the FangDash website</Step>
<Step>Signing in with their Twitch account</Step>
<Step>Entering the room code</Step>
</Steps>

Up to **4 players** can race at once.

## Stream-Friendly Features

- **Wolf skins** are visible to all players — viewers can show off their unlocks
- **Leaderboards** update in real-time, great for on-screen overlays
- **Race results** show player names and scores at the end of each race

## Future Plans

We're working on deeper Twitch integration including:

- Chat commands to join races
- Channel point redemptions for race entries
- Twitch Extension panel for one-click join
