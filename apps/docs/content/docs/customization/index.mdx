FangDash features unlockable wolf skins and achievements to keep you coming back for more.

## Wolf Skins

There are six wolf skins in total — Gray Wolf is available by default, and five additional skins (up to the legendary MrDemonWolf) must be unlocked. Each skin has a unique look and rarity level.

[View all skins](/docs/customization/skins)

## Achievements

17 achievements across 5 categories challenge you to master every aspect of the game. Earn achievements by hitting score milestones, running long distances, playing lots of games, pulling off skillful moves, and racing with others.

Some achievements unlock exclusive skins as rewards.

[View all achievements](/docs/customization/achievements)
