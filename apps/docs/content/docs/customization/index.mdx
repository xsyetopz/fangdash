FangDash features unlockable **wolf skins** and **achievements** to keep you coming back for more.

## Wolf Skins

There are **six wolf skins** in total. **Gray Wolf** is available by default, and five additional skins must be unlocked through gameplay milestones and achievements. Each skin has a unique look and rarity level.

Browse and equip skins from the [Skins gallery](/skins) in the web app.

[View all skins →](/docs/customization/skins)

## Achievements

**16 achievements** across 5 categories challenge you to master every aspect of the game. Earn achievements by hitting score milestones, running long distances, playing lots of games, pulling off skillful moves, and racing with others.

Three achievements unlock **exclusive skins** as rewards. Check your progress anytime on the [Achievements page](/achievements).

[View all achievements →](/docs/customization/achievements)

## Level & XP

As you play, every point you score earns **1 XP** toward your player level. Race placements give bonus XP (1st = 500, 2nd = 250, 3rd = 100). Your level and progress bar are visible on your [Profile](/profile).

[Learn about the level system →](/docs/gameplay/levels)
