FangDash features unlockable wolf skins and achievements to keep you coming back for more.

## Wolf Skins

There are 10 wolf skins to unlock, ranging from the default Gray Wolf to the legendary Phantom Wolf. Each skin has a unique look and rarity level.

[View all skins](/docs/customization/skins)

## Achievements

16 achievements across 5 categories challenge you to master every aspect of the game. Earn achievements by hitting score milestones, running long distances, playing lots of games, pulling off skillful moves, and racing with others.

Some achievements unlock exclusive skins as rewards.

[View all achievements](/docs/customization/achievements)
