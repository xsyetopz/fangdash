<Callout type="info">
	**TL;DR** - 6 wolf skins across 5 rarity tiers - Gray Wolf is the default — all others must be
	earned - Three skins are rewards tied to specific achievements
</Callout>

FangDash has **6 wolf skins** across 5 rarity tiers.

## All Skins

### Gray Wolf — Common

![Gray Wolf](/wolves/wolf-gray.png)

**Default** — available from the start, no unlock required.

---

### Shadow Wolf — Uncommon

![Shadow Wolf](/wolves/wolf-shadow.png)

Run **2,000m** in a single run.

---

### Fire Wolf — Rare

![Fire Wolf](/wolves/wolf-fire.png)

Score **5,000 points** in a single run.

---

### Storm Wolf — Epic

![Storm Wolf](/wolves/wolf-storm.png)

Earn the **"Obstacle Master"** achievement (clear 1,000 obstacles total).

---

### Blood Moon Wolf — Legendary

![Blood Moon Wolf](/wolves/wolf-blood-moon.png)

Score **15,000 points** in a single run.

---

### MrDemonWolf — Legendary

![MrDemonWolf](/wolves/wolf-mrdemonwolf.png)

Earn the **"Champion"** achievement (win 10 races).

## Rarity Tiers

| Tier      | Skins                        |
| --------- | ---------------------------- |
| Common    | Gray Wolf                    |
| Uncommon  | Shadow Wolf                  |
| Rare      | Fire Wolf                    |
| Epic      | Storm Wolf                   |
| Legendary | Blood Moon Wolf, MrDemonWolf |

## Equipping Skins

<Steps>
	<Step>Go to the **Skins** page at [/skins](/skins)</Step>
	<Step>
		Browse the skin gallery — unlocked skins are selectable, locked ones show their unlock condition
	</Step>
	<Step>Click any unlocked skin to equip it</Step>
</Steps>

Your skin is visible to other players in multiplayer races.
