FangDash has **6 wolf skins** across 5 rarity tiers.

## All Skins

### Gray Wolf — Common

![Gray Wolf](/wolves/wolf-gray.png)

**Default** — available from the start, no unlock required.

---

### Shadow Wolf — Uncommon

![Shadow Wolf](/wolves/wolf-shadow.png)

Run **2,000m** in a single run.

---

### Fire Wolf — Rare

![Fire Wolf](/wolves/wolf-fire.png)

Score **5,000 points** in a single run.

---

### Storm Wolf — Epic

![Storm Wolf](/wolves/wolf-storm.png)

Earn the **"Obstacle Master"** achievement (clear 1,000 obstacles total).

---

### Blood Moon Wolf — Legendary

![Blood Moon Wolf](/wolves/wolf-blood-moon.png)

Score **15,000 points** in a single run.

---

### MrDemonWolf — Legendary

![MrDemonWolf](/wolves/wolf-mrdemonwolf.png)

Earn the **"Champion"** achievement (win 10 races).

## Browsing Skins

The [Skins gallery](/skins) is a **view-only** page where you can preview all available wolf skins and see their unlock conditions. Unlocked skins are highlighted, while locked skins display what you need to do to earn them.

## Equipping Skins

Skin equipping has moved to the **Settings** page.

<Steps>
	<Step>Browse skins at [/skins](/skins) to see what's available</Step>
	<Step>Go to [/settings](/settings) to equip a skin</Step>
	<Step>Select any unlocked skin from the skin picker to equip it</Step>
</Steps>

Your skin is visible to other players in multiplayer races and on your public profile.

See [Settings](/docs/getting-started/settings) for more details.
