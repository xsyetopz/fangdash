FangDash has 10 wolf skins across 4 rarity tiers.

## Rarity Tiers

| Tier | Skins |
| --- | --- |
| Common | Gray Wolf, Brown Wolf |
| Uncommon | White Wolf, Black Wolf |
| Rare | Red Wolf, Blue Wolf, Golden Wolf |
| Legendary | Arctic Wolf, Shadow Wolf, Phantom Wolf |

## How to Unlock Skins

- **Gray Wolf** — Available from the start (default skin)
- **Other skins** — Unlocked by completing specific achievements
- Once unlocked, equip a skin from the Skins gallery in your profile

## Equipping Skins

1. Go to your **Profile**
2. Click **Skins**
3. Select any unlocked skin to equip it
4. Your skin is visible to other players in multiplayer races
