<Callout type="info">
	**TL;DR** - 10 main pages: Home, Play, Race, Leaderboard, Achievements, Skins, Settings, Profile,
	Public Profiles, Admin - Most pages require signing in with Twitch - Admin is restricted to
	admin/dev roles
</Callout>

Here's a quick tour of every page in the FangDash web app.

## Pages

| Page                | Route            | Description                                                              |
| ------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Home**            | `/`              | Landing page with a quick overview and sign-in prompt                    |
| **Play**            | `/play`          | Start a solo endless runner game                                         |
| **Race**            | `/race`          | Create or join a multiplayer race room                                   |
| **Leaderboard**     | `/leaderboard`   | Global rankings with time period and difficulty filters                  |
| **Achievements**    | `/achievements`  | View all 16 achievements and your unlock progress                        |
| **Skins**           | `/skins`         | View-only gallery of all wolf skins and their unlock conditions          |
| **Settings**        | `/settings`      | Equip skins, manage privacy, and delete your account                     |
| **Profile**         | `/profile`       | Your stats, level, recent scores, and equipped skin                      |
| **Public Profile**  | `/profile/[id]`  | View another player's public profile (linked from leaderboard)           |
| **Admin**           | `/admin`         | Player management, score moderation, and race history (admin/dev roles)  |

## Getting Around

The main navigation bar at the top links to Play, Race, Leaderboard, Achievements, and Skins. Your profile and settings are accessible from the user menu in the top right corner.

## What Requires Sign-In

You need to **sign in with Twitch** to:

- Play games (scores are tied to your account)
- Join races
- Equip skins (via Settings)
- View your profile
- Manage account settings

The **Leaderboard**, **Achievements** list, **Skins** gallery, and **Public Profiles** are viewable without signing in (subject to the player's privacy setting).
