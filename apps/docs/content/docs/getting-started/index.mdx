<Callout type="info">
	**TL;DR** - Sign in with Twitch — no password needed - Click Play to start a solo run immediately
	- Head to Multiplayer to race with others
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">**Estimated time:** ~2 minutes to start playing</Callout>

Welcome to FangDash! This guide walks you through everything you need to start playing.

<Steps>
<Step>
## Step 1 of 4 — Sign In with Twitch

FangDash uses your **Twitch account** to sign in. Click **Sign In** in the top right and authorize FangDash. We only access your username and avatar — nothing else.

</Step>

<Step>
## Step 2 of 4 — Play Your First Game

Click **Play** to start a solo game. Your wolf runs forward automatically. Jump to dodge obstacles and survive as long as possible.

</Step>

<Step>
## Step 3 of 4 — Check Your Score

After your run ends, your score is saved automatically. Open the **Leaderboard** to see how you rank against other players. Every point you scored also earned you XP toward your [level](/docs/gameplay/levels).

</Step>

<Step>
## Step 4 of 4 — Join a Race

Head to **Multiplayer** to create or join a race room. You can race with up to 3 other players at the same time. Top 3 placements earn bonus XP!

<Callout type="info">You're in! Welcome to the pack. Now go chase that high score.</Callout>

</Step>
</Steps>
