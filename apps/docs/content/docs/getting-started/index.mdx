Welcome to FangDash! This guide will walk you through everything you need to start playing.

## Step 1: Sign In with Twitch

FangDash uses your Twitch account to sign in. Click the **Sign In** button on the top right and authorize FangDash with your Twitch account. We only access your basic profile information (username and avatar).

## Step 2: Play Your First Game

Once signed in, click **Play** to start a solo game. Your wolf will automatically run forward. Your job is to dodge obstacles by jumping and timing your moves.

## Step 3: Check Your Score

After your run ends, your score is saved automatically. Check the **Leaderboard** to see how you rank against other players.

## Step 4: Join a Race

Want to compete against others? Head to the **Multiplayer** section to create or join a race room. You can race with up to 3 other players at the same time.
