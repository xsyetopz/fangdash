<Callout type="info">
**TL;DR**
- Sign in with Twitch — no password needed
- Click Play to start a solo run immediately
- Head to Multiplayer to race with others
</Callout>

Welcome to FangDash! This guide walks you through everything you need to start playing.

<Steps>
<Step>
## Sign In with Twitch

FangDash uses your **Twitch account** to sign in. Click **Sign In** in the top right and authorize FangDash. We only access your username and avatar — nothing else.
</Step>

<Step>
## Play Your First Game

Click **Play** to start a solo game. Your wolf runs forward automatically. Jump to dodge obstacles and survive as long as possible.
</Step>

<Step>
## Check Your Score

After your run ends, your score is saved automatically. Open the **Leaderboard** to see how you rank against other players.
</Step>

<Step>
## Join a Race

Head to **Multiplayer** to create or join a race room. You can race with up to 3 other players at the same time.
</Step>
</Steps>
