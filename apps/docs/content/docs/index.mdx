FangDash is a fast-paced multiplayer endless runner where players race as wolves, dodging obstacles and competing for the highest score. Built for Twitch streamers and their communities.

## What is FangDash?

FangDash lets you and your Twitch community race through procedurally generated obstacle courses as wolves. Jump over barriers, dodge hazards, and compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.

## Features

- **Endless Runner Gameplay** — Jump, dodge, and dash through procedurally generated courses
- **Multiplayer Racing** — Race up to 4 players in real-time
- **Twitch Integration** — Sign in with Twitch and race your chat
- **10 Unlockable Wolf Skins** — Earn skins through achievements and milestones
- **16 Achievements** — Track your progress across multiple categories
- **Leaderboards** — Compete globally with anti-cheat validated scores
- **Difficulty Scaling** — Speed ramps from 300 to 800 px/s as you run further
