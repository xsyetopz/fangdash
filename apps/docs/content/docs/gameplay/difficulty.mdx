---
title: Difficulty Scaling
description: How FangDash gets harder the further you run.
---

FangDash uses a progressive difficulty system. The game starts easy and ramps up as you run further.

## Speed Scaling

| Distance | Speed | Difficulty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0m | 300 px/s | Easy |
| 500m | 400 px/s | Medium |
| 1500m | 550 px/s | Hard |
| 3000m | 700 px/s | Expert |
| 5000m+ | 800 px/s | Maximum |

## What Changes

As difficulty increases:

- **Speed increases** — Your wolf runs faster, giving you less reaction time
- **Gaps narrow** — The space between obstacles gets tighter
- **Patterns get complex** — Obstacle combinations become more challenging

## Tips for High Scores

- The first 500 meters are free points. Don't take unnecessary risks
- Medium difficulty is where most players start losing lives. Practice here
- At expert and above, every jump needs to be precise. Watch the patterns
