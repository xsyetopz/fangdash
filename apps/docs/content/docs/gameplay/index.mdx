<Callout type="info">
**TL;DR**
- Your wolf runs automatically — you only control jumping
- Jump over obstacles; the game speeds up the further you go
- Score points for surviving (10/sec) and clearing obstacles (50 each)
</Callout>

FangDash is an **endless runner**. Your wolf runs forward automatically, and your goal is to survive as long as possible while racking up the highest score.

## The Basics

| # | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | **Your wolf runs automatically** — you don't control speed |
| 2 | **Jump to avoid obstacles** — tap or press to jump over barriers |
| 3 | **The game gets harder** — speed increases and gaps narrow the further you go |
| 4 | **Score points** — earn points based on time survived and obstacles dodged |

## Obstacle Types

You'll encounter 4 obstacle types as you run:

| Obstacle | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| **Rock** | Low and wide — easy single jump |
| **Log** | Medium height — standard timing |
| **Bush** | Low and fast-approaching |
| **Spike** | Tall — requires careful timing |

Obstacles are **procedurally generated**, so every run is different. In multiplayer, all players face the same layout thanks to seeded random generation.

## Tips for New Players

- **Don't panic** — The game starts slow. Use the early section to get comfortable
- **Watch ahead** — Look at what's coming, not just what's right in front of you
- **Practice timing** — Jumps have a fixed height and duration. Learn the rhythm
- **Play more games** — Achievements unlock based on games played, not just score
