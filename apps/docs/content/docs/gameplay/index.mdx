FangDash is an endless runner game. Your wolf runs forward automatically, and your goal is to survive as long as possible while racking up the highest score.

## The Basics

1. **Your wolf runs automatically** — You don't control the speed
2. **Jump to avoid obstacles** — Tap or press to jump over barriers
3. **The game gets harder** — Speed increases and gaps narrow the further you go
4. **Score points** — You earn points based on distance traveled and obstacles dodged

## Obstacles

You'll encounter different types of obstacles as you run:

- **Low barriers** — Jump over these
- **Tall barriers** — Time your jump carefully
- **Mixed patterns** — Combinations that require quick reactions

Obstacles are procedurally generated, so every run is different. In multiplayer, all players face the same layout thanks to seeded random generation.

## Tips for New Players

- **Don't panic** — The game starts slow. Use the early section to get comfortable
- **Watch ahead** — Look at what's coming, not just what's right in front of you
- **Practice timing** — The jump has a fixed height and duration. Learn the timing
- **Play more games** — Achievements unlock based on games played, not just score
