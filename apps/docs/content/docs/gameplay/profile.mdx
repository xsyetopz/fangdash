Your profile page shows everything about your FangDash career. Visit it at [/profile](/profile).

## Performance Stats

Your profile displays a stats grid with six key metrics:

| Stat                  | What It Tracks                          |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Total Distance**    | Combined meters run across all games    |
| **High Score**        | Your single best score ever             |
| **Win Rate**          | Percentage of races you've won          |
| **Obstacles Cleared** | Total obstacles dodged across all games |
| **Games Played**      | Total number of games completed         |
| **Total Score**       | Combined score across all games         |

## Level & XP

A progress bar shows your current **level** and how far you are to the next one. Every point you score earns 1 XP, and race placements give bonus XP.

See [Level System](/docs/gameplay/levels) for the full breakdown.

## Honor Badges

Up to **12 achievement badges** are displayed on your profile. The slots are filled with your most recently unlocked achievements first, then padded with locked (unearned) achievements to reach 12. This way you always see a full grid — your latest wins up front, with goals still to chase.

See [Achievements](/docs/customization/achievements) for the full list.

## Recent Scorelines

Your last 8 scores are displayed with **trend arrows** showing whether each score was higher or lower than the one before it. This helps you track whether you're improving over time.

## Equipped Skin

Your currently equipped wolf skin is displayed on your profile. Other players can see it too.

Change your skin anytime from [Settings](/settings).

## Public Profiles

Every player has a public profile at `/profile/[id]` that other players can view. Public profiles are linked from the leaderboard — click any player's name to visit their profile.

Public profiles display:

- Player stats (distance, high score, games played, etc.)
- Achievements earned
- Top scores
- Equipped skin

### Share Profile

On your own profile page, a **Share Profile** button lets you copy a direct link to your public profile to share with others.

### Privacy Toggle

If you prefer to keep your profile private, you can disable public visibility from [Settings](/settings). When privacy is enabled:

- Your public profile page shows a "This profile is private" notice
- Your name on the leaderboard will not link to your profile

The privacy toggle is labeled **"Allow others to view my profile"** and can be changed at any time. See [Settings](/docs/getting-started/settings) for details.
