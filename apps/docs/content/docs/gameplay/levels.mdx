<Callout type="info">
	**TL;DR** - Every point you score = 1 XP - Race bonuses: 1st place = 500 XP, 2nd = 250 XP, 3rd =
	100 XP - Levels follow a cubic curve — early levels are fast, later ones take real dedication
</Callout>

FangDash has a **level system** that tracks your overall progress. The higher your level, the more experienced you are.

## How You Earn XP

| Source             | XP Earned             |
| ------------------ | --------------------- |
| **Score points**   | 1 XP per point scored |
| **1st place race** | 500 bonus XP          |
| **2nd place race** | 250 bonus XP          |
| **3rd place race** | 100 bonus XP          |
| **4th place race** | No bonus XP           |

Every point you score in a game — whether solo or multiplayer — counts as 1 XP. Race placements give you a bonus on top of that.

## Level Curve

Levels use a **cubic formula**: `totalXpForLevel(n) = 5 × (n - 1)³`

Early levels come quickly. Higher levels take significantly more XP.

| Level | Total XP Needed | XP for This Level |
| ----- | --------------- | ----------------- |
| 1     | 0               | —                 |
| 2     | 5               | 5                 |
| 3     | 40              | 35                |
| 5     | 320             | 185               |
| 10    | 3,645           | 1,085             |
| 15    | 13,720          | 2,735             |
| 20    | 34,295          | 5,135             |
| 30    | 121,945         | 12,185            |
| 50    | 588,245         | 35,285            |

## Your Progress Bar

Your current level and XP progress are visible on your **Profile** page. The progress bar shows how far you are toward the next level.

Check your profile anytime at [/profile](/profile).

<Callout type="info">
	XP is calculated from your **total score** plus any race placement bonuses. It updates
	automatically after every game.
</Callout>
