## How Scoring Works

Your score in FangDash is based on how far you run and how long you survive.

- **Distance** — The primary factor in your score. The further you run, the higher your score
- **Duration** — How long your run lasted (in seconds)

## Score Validation

FangDash uses server-side anti-cheat validation. When you submit a score:

1. The server checks that your score is physically possible given the game's max speed
2. Scores that exceed the maximum theoretical rate (15 points per second) are rejected
3. Your score is recorded along with your distance and duration for verification

## Leaderboards

The global leaderboard shows the top scores across all players. Your personal best scores are also tracked on your profile.

Leaderboard rankings update in real-time as new scores are submitted.
