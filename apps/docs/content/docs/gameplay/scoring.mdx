## Score Formula

Your score in FangDash is calculated from two sources:

- **Time survived** — 10 points per second
- **Obstacles cleared** — 50 points per obstacle dodged

Your total score is the sum of both. Distance is also tracked separately at a reduced multiplier for achievements and leaderboard stats.

## Score Validation

FangDash uses server-side anti-cheat validation. When you submit a score:

1. The server checks that your score does not exceed 15 points per second (the maximum theoretical rate)
2. Scores that exceed this limit are automatically rejected
3. Your score, distance, and duration are all recorded for verification
4. After a valid score is saved, the server automatically checks for any newly earned achievements and skin unlocks

## Leaderboards

The global leaderboard shows the top scores across all players. Your personal best scores are also tracked on your profile.

Leaderboard rankings update in real-time as new scores are submitted.
