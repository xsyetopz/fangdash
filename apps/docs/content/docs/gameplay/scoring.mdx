<Callout type="info">
**TL;DR**
- Earn 10 pts/sec for surviving + 50 pts per obstacle cleared
- All scores are validated server-side against the theoretical maximum
- Invalid scores are automatically rejected
</Callout>

## Score Formula

Your score comes from two sources:

| Source | Points |
| --- | --- |
| **Time survived** | 10 points per second |
| **Obstacles cleared** | 50 points per obstacle dodged |

Your total score is the sum of both. Distance is tracked separately for achievements and leaderboard stats.

## Score Validation

FangDash uses **server-side anti-cheat validation**. When you submit a score:

<Steps>
<Step>The server checks that your score does not exceed `(seconds × 10) + (obstacles × 50)` — the maximum possible for the given run duration and obstacle count</Step>
<Step>Scores above this limit are automatically rejected</Step>
<Step>Your score, distance, and duration are all recorded for verification</Step>
<Step>After a valid score is saved, the server automatically checks for newly earned achievements and skin unlocks</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
Submitted scores that exceed the theoretical maximum for your run duration and obstacle count will be silently rejected. No error is shown — the score simply won't appear on the leaderboard.
</Callout>

## Leaderboards

The **global leaderboard** shows the top scores across all players. Your personal best scores are tracked on your profile.

Leaderboard rankings update in real-time as new scores are submitted.
